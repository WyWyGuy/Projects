    Released in 2017, "The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild" is the newest game in the Zelda series. Following Link on a quest to stop Calamity Ganon by recruiting the four Divine Beasts, you must rescue Zelda to beat the game.
    Find the Master Sword in the Korok Forest and regain memories as you travel throughout Hyrule. By climbing discovered towers, you can gain map pieces and discover hidden locations. Get all 120 shrines and all 900 koroks to complete the game!
    If you buy the DLC, you will gain access to a bonus divine beast with more memories, more shrines and challenges, a trial to upgrade the Master Sword, and a bunch of hidden new costumes! Any way you play it, Breath of the Wild is an amazing and beautiful game.
